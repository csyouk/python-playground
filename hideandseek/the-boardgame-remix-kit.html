The Boardgame Remix Kit

Turning games you own into new games you'll love

The Boardgame Remix Kit is a a different way to use your old board games, giving you ways to use the boards and pieces of popular classics to create a host of new, original games. Described by Boingboing as “the most insanely clever Christmas gift idea of the year… It’s fun, it’s beautifully designed, and it will save the sanity of anyone stuck indoors over the holidays”, it’s available as an iPhone app, eBook, a paper book and a limited-edition set of rulecards.

The kit contains 27 tweaks, new games, and mash-ups, designed to cater to every conceivable combination of mood and circumstance. Some are silly and social, like Judy Garland on the Moon with a Bassoon. Some are intense and involving, like Zombie Mansion. Some are creative and strategic like Them’s Fightin’ Words.

The Boardgame Remix Kit is 100% unofficial and unaffiliated with any board game manufacturer. Visit the website to read an excerpt and find out more.
